Homemade Oat Milk

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 4 cups of Water
  • 1-2 tsp of Maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender
    1. Plug blender in
    2. Turn blender on high speed setting
  2. Blend on high for about 2 minutes
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
    4. If you let it sit for more than days, it will go bad.
